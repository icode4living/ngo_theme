About Us

The JOHN PAUL OKONMA Foundation is a distinguished nonprofit organization dedicated to positively impacting the lives of people in Nigeria and Africa as a whole. We firmly believe in the power of compassion and unity, and our primary objective is to uplift the underprivileged and bring joy to their lives. We strive to extend our reach each year, empowering individuals to become self-sufficient. By collaborating with our valued partners, we have successfully launched numerous projects aimed at assisting those in need. As a renowned NGO headquartered in the vibrant Delta state of the Southern region of Nigeria, our organization comprises dedicated professionals from diverse backgrounds who pool their resources and expertise to undertake commendable projects.
